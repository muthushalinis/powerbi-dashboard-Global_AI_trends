This project presents an interactive dashboard analysing the global impact of AI adoption across countries and industries.
The dashboard focuses on understanding how AI tools are being used worldwide and how adoption relates to factors such as consumer trust, job impact, revenue, and collaboration.

It covers:

- Country-wise usage of leading AI tools  
- Global consumer trust in AI and AI content volume  
- AI adoption trends over time and their relation to job loss  
- Industry-level filtering to compare AI impact across sectors  
- AI market share comparison across countries  
- Geographic distribution of AI trust and adoption  

Key metrics include:

- Total consumer trust in AI  
- AI content generation volume  
- Adoption rates, job loss percentages, revenue, and collaboration trends  
Interactive slicers allow analysis by industry and country.

Tools used:

- Power BI  
- DAX  
- Data modelling and visualisation
